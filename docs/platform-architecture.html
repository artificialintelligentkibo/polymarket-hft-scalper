SNIPER — Binance-Led Momentum Entry

Detects Binance price movement before Polymarket adjusts. Crosses the spread to buy the expected winner. Fast scalp exit on profit or reversal.

Entry: SNIPER_BUY (priority 1200, urgency: cross)

1
Binance data available → reject if no data
2
Direction not FLAT → reject if direction === 'FLAT'
3
Minimum Binance move → reject if movePct < SNIPER_MIN_BINANCE_MOVE_PCT (0.035%)
4
Velocity confirmation → reject if velocity < SNIPER_MIN_VELOCITY_PCT_PER_SEC (0.002)
5
Runaway guard → reject if move too large (prevents chasing)
6
Coin losing cooldown → reject if recent loss on this coin
7
Orderbook liquidity ≥ MIN_LIQUIDITY_USD ($500)
8
Balance pre-flight → reject if can't afford entry
9
Slot warmup passed: elapsed > SNIPER_SLOT_WARMUP_MS (5s)
10
Not too late: remaining > SNIPER_EXIT_BEFORE_END_MS (30s)
11
Expected winner resolved (UP→YES or DOWN→NO)
12
Market cooldown: elapsed > SNIPER_COOLDOWN_MS (3s)
13
No existing position in opposite direction
14
Best ask available and in range: SNIPER_MIN_ENTRY_PRICE (0.03) to SNIPER_MAX_ENTRY_PRICE (0.40)
15
Fair value edge: impliedFV - bestAsk*(1+fee) ≥ SNIPER_MIN_EDGE_AFTER_FEES (0.025)
16
PM lag: |FV - bestAsk| ≥ SNIPER_MIN_PM_LAG (0.03)
17
Correlation limit: sameDirectionEntries < SNIPER_MAX_CONCURRENT_SAME_DIRECTION (2)
✓
SIGNAL: SNIPER_BUY — cross spread, buy at bestAsk, shares: 6 (base) or 6 (strong if move > threshold)

OBI ENGINE — Order Book Imbalance

Core strategy from vague-sourdough analysis. Enters the thin side of an imbalanced orderbook, waits for rebalance. Layer 2: posts maker ask after entry.

Entry: OBI_ENTRY_BUY (priority 900, urgency: passive/cross)

1
OBI_ENGINE_ENABLED=true
2
Slot warmup passed (5s since slot start)
3
Not too late: remaining > OBI_STOP_ENTRY_BEFORE_END_MS (60s)
4
Imbalance analysis: compute thinRatio = thinSideDepth / thickSideDepth for YES and NO
5
Imbalance threshold: thinRatio < OBI_ENTRY_IMBALANCE_RATIO (0.20) → reject if book balanced
6
Thin side depth ≥ OBI_THIN_THRESHOLD_USD ($8) → reject if thin side too empty
7
Total liquidity ≥ OBI_MIN_LIQUIDITY_USD ($500) → reject if illiquid market
8
Price range: bestAsk ≤ OBI_MAX_ENTRY_PRICE (0.49)
9
No existing position on this market
10
Binance gate: not runaway, not contra-direction, alignment if required
11
Market cooldown: elapsed > OBI_COOLDOWN_MS (5s)
12
Coin losing cooldown (no recent loss on same coin)
13
Balance pre-flight check
14
Shadow mode check → if shadow, log only, return []
✓
SIGNAL: OBI_ENTRY_BUY — buy thin side at bestAsk - 1 tick (top of bid, maker). Shares: 6, max position: 12

Phase 1: VS_MM_BID / VS_MM_ASK (priority ~800, urgency: passive)

1
VS_ENGINE_ENABLED=true and not shadow mode
2
Phase = PASSIVE_MM (between warmup and T-30s)
3
Calculate fair value: FV = Φ((spot - strike) / (σ · √(t/300)))
4
Direction threshold: |FV - 0.50| ≥ VS_MIN_DIRECTION_THRESHOLD (0.005) → reject if FV too flat
5
No existing position, below max shares (VS_MM_MAX_POSITION_SHARES = 12)

Cooldown: elapsed > VS_COOLDOWN_MS (8s)
✓
VS_MM_BID at FV - VS_MM_SPREAD_CENTS (passive maker bid)
✓
VS_MM_ASK at min(entry + VS_MAKER_ASK_MAX_EDGE, VS_TARGET_EXIT_PRICE)

LOTTERY — Cheap Reversal Ticket

After Sniper entry, buys a cheap ticket on the opposite outcome. If market reverses, pays big. Max risk $3 per ticket.

Entry: LOTTERY_BUY (priority 100, urgency: passive)

1
LOTTERY_LAYER_ENABLED=true
2
Trigger: LOTTERY_ONLY_AFTER_SNIPER=true → only after SNIPER_BUY fill
3
Outcome = opposite of sniper entry (sniper bought YES → lottery buys NO)
4
No existing lottery ticket on this outcome
5
Slot limit: tickets < LOTTERY_MAX_PER_SLOT (1)
6
Price in range: LOTTERY_MIN_CENTS (0.12) to LOTTERY_MAX_CENTS (0.18)
7
Risk check: shares * price ≤ LOTTERY_MAX_RISK_USDC ($3)
✓
SIGNAL: LOTTERY_BUY — passive maker bid at min(targetPrice, bestAsk - tick)

MARKET MAKER — Post-Fill Quoting

Reactive layer. After Sniper fills, posts maker quotes to improve exit price. Gated by OBI imbalance filter.

Activation Flow

1
MARKET_MAKER_MODE=true and DYNAMIC_QUOTING_ENABLED=true
2
MM_AUTO_ACTIVATE_AFTER_SNIPER=true → triggers on SNIPER_BUY fill
3
Grace window: MM_POST_SNIPER_GRACE_WINDOW_MS (15s) after fill
4
OBI filter gate: if ORDER_BOOK_IMBALANCE_ENABLED, checks imbalance allows quoting
5
Concurrent limit: MM_MAX_CONCURRENT_MARKETS (1)
6
Not too late: remaining > MM_STOP_NEW_ENTRIES_BEFORE_END_MS (90s)
✓
MM_QUOTE_BID / MM_QUOTE_ASK — posts bid/ask around position with MM_MAKER_MIN_EDGE (0.003)

Polymarket Fee Formula

fee = feeRate × min(price, 1 - price) × shares

Maker fee: 0%
Standard taker fee: 2%
High-fee taker: 3.15%
